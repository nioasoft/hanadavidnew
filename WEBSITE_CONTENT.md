## About Page Content

### English Version

#### Main Story

I'm Hana David, and I bring a lifelong passion for languages and teaching to every lesson.

Born and raised in the United States, I developed a deep love for reading and languages from an early age. I studied history, foreign languages (French, Latin, Russian, and Spanish), and creative writing at the University of Memphis, while also volunteering to teach English to new immigrants.

After making aliyah to Israel in 2019, I pursued my dream of becoming a professional English teacher. In January 2025, I completed the prestigious CELTA certification (Certificate in English Language Teaching to Adults), where I was mentored by experienced EFL teachers and taught students from around the world.

During my training, I received high marks for:
- Creating authentic, engaging lesson materials
- Building strong connections with students
- Designing relevant, practical lessons that make learning enjoyable

Now, I'm dedicated to helping you achieve your English goals through personalized, one-on-one instruction tailored to your specific needs and learning style.

#### Teaching Philosophy

Every student is unique, with their own goals, learning style, and pace. That's why I don't believe in one-size-fits-all teaching. Instead, I create a completely personalized learning experience for each student, using materials and methods that are relevant to your life and interests. Whether you're preparing for a business presentation, planning a trip abroad, or simply want to feel more confident speaking English, we'll work together to get you there.
